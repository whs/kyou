To add a video, enter the video ID in the Video ID box and click

For example, the video https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oHg5SJYRHA0 have the video ID oHg5SJYRHA0 and video https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u8sqeO0-duA&feature=youtu.be have the video ID u8sqeO0-duA. If you enter the video ID correctly, the video name should be shown.

Parameters

Autohide
  • Only progress bar: While playing, hide the red progress bar.
  • Controls: While playing, hide both progress bar and control buttons.
  • Disable: While playing, always show both progress bar and control buttons
Autoplay
Load and play the clip automatically. Not recommended as auto sounds may annoy some readers.
Controls
Show the control buttons. If disabled, user still can play/pause video by pressing space.
Annotations
Show video annotations, a YouTube feature.
Loop
When video ends, replays
Hide YT logo
Hide the YouTube logo while playing. A YouTube mark still show before video starts
Show related videos
Show related videos from YouTube after video ended
Show video info
Show video's name before playing
Theme
Dark is the standard YouTube's theme, while White is the inverted theme.
Start Time
Start playing the first video from this second. Will snap to the nearest keyframe due to technical limitations.
