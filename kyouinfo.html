Kyou Books, made interactive.

Currently in private beta.

Kyou is a product of the research that we did before the creation of CoreAnime.
E-Books should be interacted the way you interact with computers and tablets.

Easy-to-use editor

Web-based, cloud powered

No installation. Just sign in and you're ready to go

Forget the floppy disk

Saving is so 1997. In 2012, we use auto-saving and versioning.

Hassle-free distribution

Kyou outputs in Chrome Web Store and PhoneGap:Build friendly format. Just upload it and you're done.

Coding free

Want a 3D box? Few clicks and you got it. No coding.

But if you insist

Kyou allow you to supply custom CSS and JavaScript.

CSS?

If you don't know CSS. No problem. Kyou provides popular CSS attributes as boxes. You don't even need to know which unit to use.

Social + Work

Invite your friend to work with you! Kyou supports sharing of project to multiple users.

WYSIWYG

Live preview your edits. Also, preview it read-only on your devices or with your friends.

Customizable

Kyou is powered by Backbone and jQuery. Write your own widgets and layouts easily.

Interactive book

Made for Chrome, and iPad

Kyou use standard HTML5 with WebKit extensions to support both Chrome, iPads and possibly all WebKit-based browsers.

Interactive

No, this is not your ordinary flashy flip books. We have real interactive image galleries, image hovers, videos (yes, YouTube!) or even a real 3D box as seen in CoreAnime 1
